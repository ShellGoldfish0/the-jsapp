## The "Why" Behind the Blog
Most people keep their hobbies and their careers separate. I’m doing the opposite. This blog exists to document my journey through **Cybersecurity** while showcasing the high-level coordination required in **Destiny 2**. 

## The Mission: Clearing a Day 1 Raid
One of my primary goals this year is to clear a **Day 1 Raid**. For those who don't play, this is the ultimate "stress test"—limited power levels, unknown mechanics, and a 48 hour clock. Unlike traditional raids, these are set at a fixed power with higher level enemy AI, so we are always underleveled. The other thing is that it is a 6-man activity. So, everyone has to be operating at peak efficiency in order to get the clear. Clearing a day one results in a exclusive emblem, a gaurenteed drop of the raids exotic weapon, and if you are first, the title of world's first and a WWE style belt.
